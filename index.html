CHAPTER 03 — THE MOST CONTESTED CLAIM

The Stealth Equation

The Su-57 incorporates planform alignment, serrated edges, radar-absorbent materials, and internal weapons bays. However, every public figure regarding its radar cross-section is an estimate — derived from patents, photography, and analyst interpretation. No independently verified measurement has been released.
