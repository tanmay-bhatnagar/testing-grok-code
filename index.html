CHAPTER 03 — THE MOST CONTESTED CLAIM

The Stealth Equation

The Su-57 incorporates planform alignment, serrated edges, radar-absorbent materials (RAM), and internal weapons bays. These features achieve a major reduction from fourth-generation baselines (Su-27 family often cited 4–20 m²).

Every public RCS number is an estimate. The primary documented source is Russian patent №2502643 (Sukhoi), which described a design goal of reducing average RCS to roughly 0.1–1 m² versus a Su-27 baseline of ~10–15 m².

CRITICAL CAVEAT

No independently verified public radar range measurements exist for the production Su-57. Western analyst estimates for frontal aspect typically range 0.1–1 m². Rear aspect is significantly higher. Low-frequency radars remain a notable vulnerability due to aircraft size.
